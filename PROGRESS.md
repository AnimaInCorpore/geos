- 2026-03-12: Root-caused the Phase 4 SIO stall to three missing hardware behaviors in jsA8E's POKEY serial emulation (`third_party/A8E/jsA8E/js/core/io.js`). (1) Writing SEROUT ($D20D) did not clear IRQST bits 3 (XMTDON) and 4 (serial output data needed) back to "not pending."
